About the Owner:
Alexander Chan is the owner, investor, and general manager of Platinum Housing® at UCLA. He attended UCLA and realized the need for affordable and quality housing near campus. In his time at UCLA, he played the tenor saxophone in the UCLA Marching Band. He also is a big fan of Indian food. Rent from Platinum Housing® for a joyous and affordable living experience.  

About Platinum Housing®
Platinum Housing® aims to achieve 3 goals: affordability, quality and safety.

Our number one goal is to maintain affordable rents for all registered UCLA students. To do this, all full-time students will be eligible for a student rate, that is generally about 20% below market rents.

Platinum Housing® maintains high quality rental units by consistently upgrading units and renovating the complex.

Platinum Housing® maintains a safe enviornment by providing round-the-clock security for all its residents.-
